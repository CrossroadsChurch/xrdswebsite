Ministries

We serve and minister in a variety of ways, and believe that the most important mission field is the one on our doorstep:

Community outreach:

    Charles and Bev are active members of Christians Together in Mission, (TiM).

    Charles is the project developer for Whitley SALT (Social Action Linked Together) combining the services and activities of several Christian charities and churches under a single umbrella.

Befriending:

    Bev is the coordinator for TiM Friends, a befriending service which reaches out to elderly and isolated people in Burghfield, Mortimer, Sulhamstead, Ufton Nervet and beyond.

Mental health:

    Charles is the chaplain, operations manager and a group facilitator at Still the Hunger, a Christian mental health recovery programme
